title: Apple Wallet Pass
description: Add your Solar Network membership card to Apple Wallet
---

Solar Network supports Apple Wallet integration, allowing you to add a membership card to the Wallet app on your iPhone.

## Downloading Your Pass

1. Go to your account settings on a device with Apple Wallet support
2. Download your member pass (.pkpass file)
3. Open the file to add it to your Wallet

## What's on the Pass

- Your account information
- Current Stellar Plan tier (if active)
- Member serial number

## PassKit Web Service

The pass supports automatic updates via Apple's PassKit web service. When your membership status changes, your pass updates automatically in the Wallet app.

## Requirements

- iOS device with Apple Wallet support
- Active Solar Network account
