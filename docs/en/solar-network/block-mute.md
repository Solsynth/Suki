---
title: Block & Mute
description: Manage your interactions with other users
---

Solar Network provides two mechanisms to control your interactions with other users: **Block** and **Mute**.

## Block

Blocking a user creates a **mutual** interaction restriction. Neither you nor the blocked user can see or interact with each other.

When you block someone:

- Their posts are hidden from your feed and timeline
- You cannot reply to, react to, forward, boost, or award their posts
- Their messages and notifications are suppressed
- Direct messages between you are blocked
- They are hidden from search and autocomplete
- Follow requests between you are blocked

## Mute

Muting a user is **one-directional**. You stop seeing the muted user's content, but they are unaffected.

When you mute someone:

- Their posts are hidden from your feed and timeline
- You stop receiving notifications from them
- They can still interact with your content normally

## Key Differences

| | Block | Mute |
|---|-------|------|
| Direction | Mutual | One-directional |
| They see your content | No | Yes |
| You see their content | No | No |
| Direct messages | Blocked | Allowed |
| Notifications | Suppressed | Suppressed |

## Managing Your Block/Mute List

You can view and manage your blocked and muted users in your account settings. You can unblock or unmute users at any time.
