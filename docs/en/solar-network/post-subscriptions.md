---
title: Post Subscriptions
description: Get notified about new posts and updates
---

Post Subscriptions let you subscribe to notifications about categories, tags, collections, or individual posts.

## Collection Subscriptions

Subscribe to a post collection to receive notifications when new posts are added:

- Get notified when a collection you follow gets a new post
- Manage subscriptions from the collection page

## Category & Tag Subscriptions

Subscribe to categories or tags to receive notifications when new matching posts are published.

## Per-Post Subscriptions

Subscribe to an individual post to receive notifications when it receives:

- New reactions
- Boosts
- Significant edits

This is useful for following posts you're interested in without subscribing to the entire publisher.

## Managing Subscriptions

You can view and manage all your post subscriptions in your notification settings.
