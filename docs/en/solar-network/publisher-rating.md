Publisher Rating is a reputation score assigned to each publisher. It affects how your content is ranked and discovered on Solar Network.

## How Rating Works

Every publisher starts with a **base rating of 100**. The rating changes based on your post engagement:

- **Positive reactions** to your posts increase your rating
- **Negative reactions** decrease your rating
- **Awards** on your posts also contribute

Rating changes are recorded as individual records and are permanent — they do not decay over time.

## Rating Levels

| Level | Score Range |
|-------|-------------|
| Low | < 100 |
| Normal | 100 - 200 |
| Good | 200 - 300 |
| Excellent | ≥ 300 |

## Impact on Your Content

- **Timeline ranking:** Higher-rated publishers receive a visibility boost in the feed
- **Discovery:** Well-rated publishers are more likely to be recommended
- **Profile display:** Your rating level is visible on your publisher profile

## How to Improve Your Rating

1. **Publish quality content** that receives positive engagement
2. **Stay active** and maintain consistent posting
3. **Avoid violations** that lead to penalties
4. **Engage positively** with the community

Unlike the Social Trust Score (which is account-level), Publisher Rating is specific to each publisher you manage.
