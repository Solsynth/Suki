---
title: Calendar Events
description: Create and manage personal calendar events
---

Calendar Events let you create personal events with visibility controls, viewable in a merged calendar alongside check-ins, status updates, and notable days.

## Creating an Event

Events support:

- **Title and description**
- **Start and end time**
- **Location**
- **All-day events**

## Visibility

| Level | Who Can See |
|-------|-------------|
| Public | Everyone |
| Friends | Only your friends |
| Private | Only you |

## Recurring Events

Events can be set to repeat:

- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Yearly

## Calendar View

Your calendar merges:

- Your personal events
- Daily check-in results
- Status updates
- Notable days and holidays

All times are stored in UTC and converted by your client to your local timezone.
